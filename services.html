Services

Barbara offers services to help you along your writing journey!

Short-Term Mentoring

Assistance with branding and platform building, including reviewing ad copy, promotional materials, and developing marketing strategies. As well as providing support for writing and revising book proposals, along with marketing advice, guidance on finding legitimate publishers, and self-publishing options.

Rate: $35/hr

notes
Long-Term Mentoring

Offers mentoring to help authors organize their material and access resources for research, editing, and marketing, helping to save time and accelerate the process. Advising on publishing options, including whether to seek an agent or approach small publishers. For a set-up fee, Barbara reviews your project, including your book's cover, blurb, and first chapter, and helps with promotional materials and marketing strategies, tailored to your target audience, even before the book is finished.

Rate: $75/hr

contract_edit
Contract Review

Barbara Florio Graham will review your publishing contract, commenting on the contract terms, flagging problems, suggesting modifications, and letting you know if there are any clauses that you should refer to a lawyer. Barbara is not a lawyer, and she is not offering legal services. But having worked as a law clerk she understands legal language, and has considerable expertise in contract terms and copyright.

Rate: $75/hr

Online Course

This PDF contains course materials with 8 lectures, suggested assignments to do at your own pace, as well as new and updated bonus material. No feedback, as that's only available in the complete Tapping Your Innate Creativity course.

Rate: $80/hr
